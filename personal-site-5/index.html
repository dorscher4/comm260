Entertainment

Watching video games is no different than watching sports! Today there are so many different ways to consumer entertainment in regards to video games. From watching professionals play, to funny videos on YouTube and live streams on platforms like Twitch, there is something for everyone to watch!

History

The precense of video games can trace back to the 50s, where new computer scientists created their own computer games for research or for fun. A couple decades later, video games became more popular as started being massed produced. Today, we are able to play games with people across the world or even in a virtual reality. Video gaming has come so far since their discrete origin in the 1950s.

Community

Getting into gaming is very fun and rewarding. It is also relatively easy as there are many different types of games for everyone to enjoy. There is also evidence of certain benefits from gaming, such as improved vision, attention, and decision making.
