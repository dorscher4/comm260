Entertainment

Today, entertainment in the form of video games is at an all time high. Not only are there next-gen consoles that are unlike any we've seen before, but there are a large number of ways for people to consume this content other than actually playing these games for themselves.

Most people today have heard of YouTube, but Twitch is a platform that is still growing. It is a place where people can live stream content, a majority of which being video games, and interact with the people watching in real time. Many visit the site to watch and talk to their favorite creators from YouTube or streamers that have made their name on Twitch itself. Others may come to watch the competitiveness and the skill involve in various eSports.

That's right eSports. Professional video game players. There are many sponsored professional gaming teams and international events that take place all the time, just like football for instance. There have even been eSports events shown live on channels like ESPN! For more information about entertainment, go to this page page.
